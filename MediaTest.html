What is Origami?

Origami is the traditional Japanese art of paper folding. The goal is to transform a flat sheet of paper into a finished sculpture through folding techniques, without cutting or gluing. It can range from simple designs, like paper cranes or boats, to complex and intricate models. The practice often involves a lot of precision and creativity, and it's appreciated both as an art form and as a way to develop spatial reasoning skills.
